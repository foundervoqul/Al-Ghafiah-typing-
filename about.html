Our Company Story

AL GHAFIAH — A Name Built on Trust

AL GHAFIAH has been serving customers in Rolla, Sharjah for over 22 years with trusted typing, translation, and document support. Founded by MD Rafi, the company supports individuals and businesses with accurate document preparation, application assistance, translation support, copying, scanning, and office document services.

Every service is built around speed, professionalism, clarity, customer trust, and practical completion. Whether you need a simple photocopy or a complex immigration application, we handle it with equal care and precision.

From our home at Rolla, Sharjah, we serve the diverse UAE community — speaking Arabic, English, Urdu, and Hindi to make our services truly accessible to all.
