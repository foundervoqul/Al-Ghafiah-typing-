SERVICES

What typing services do you offer?+
We offer professional typing in Arabic, English, Urdu, and Hindi. Our typing services include general documents, applications, forms, quotations, agreements, invoices, and official government submissions.
What translation services do you provide?+
We provide accurate translation services between Arabic, English, Urdu, and Hindi. Our translations are professionally done with attention to accuracy, context, and formatting. We handle legal, personal, and business documents.
Do you handle official government forms?+
Yes. We specialize in typing official UAE government forms and applications including Emirates ID, immigration forms, trade license applications, gate pass typing, and more. Our team is experienced with all UAE government formats.

SHARJAH PORTS GATE PASS

What is Sharjah Ports Gate Pass Typing?+
This is our most requested service. We type the official Gate Pass applications required for access to Sharjah Port facilities. Our 22 years of experience ensures error-free, properly formatted submissions every time.
How quickly can you complete Gate Pass typing?+
Gate Pass typing is typically completed quickly. Contact us via WhatsApp (055-5533183) with your requirements for current turnaround times. We prioritize urgent requests whenever possible.
What documents do I need to bring for Gate Pass typing?+
Requirements vary by application type. Generally you will need your Emirates ID, relevant vehicle or company documents, and any port-specific reference numbers. Contact us beforehand via WhatsApp and we will tell you exactly what to bring.

EMIRATES ID & IMMIGRATION

Do you help with Emirates ID applications?+
Yes. We assist with new Emirates ID applications, renewals, and related form typing. We ensure all information is correctly entered to avoid rejection or delays. Bring your passport, visa, and any existing ID documents.
Can you help with immigration and visa applications?+
Yes. We assist with typing immigration applications, residence visa forms, and related documents. Our experienced team knows the required formats and helps ensure your application is complete and accurate.

TRADE LICENSE & BUSINESS

Do you help with trade license applications?+
Yes. We handle Trade License application typing for new businesses and renewals in Sharjah. We also prepare MOA (Memorandum of Association) and LLC Agreement documents. Our team understands the requirements for different business types.
Can you type MOA and LLC agreements?+
Yes. We type Memorandum of Association (MOA) and LLC agreements professionally, ensuring they meet all required legal formats. We work with your legal advisor's content to produce accurately formatted documents.

ATTESTATION & CERTIFICATES

Do you handle Indian certificate attestation?+
Yes. We assist with preparing and organizing Indian certificates for the attestation process. We help ensure documents are in the correct format and order. Contact us to discuss your specific attestation requirements.

COPY · LAMINATION · SCANNING

Do you offer photocopy services?+
Yes. We provide high-quality photocopying for all document types — A4, A3, and more. Black & white and color copies available. Walk in or contact us in advance for large volume orders.
Do you offer lamination services?+
Yes. We provide professional lamination services for certificates, ID cards, important documents, and more. Various sizes available. Lamination protects your documents from wear, moisture, and damage.
Do you scan documents and send via email?+
Yes. We offer scanning and email services. Bring your documents and we will scan them and send them to any email address you provide. High-resolution scanning available for clear, professional digital copies.

RUBBER STAMPS

Yes. We produce custom rubber stamps for businesses and individuals. Provide us your stamp text and design, and we will create a professional quality rubber stamp for you. Contact us for pricing and turnaround times.
